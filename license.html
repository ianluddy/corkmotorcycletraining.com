Motorcycle Licenses

You must hold a current driving license or a learner permit before driving any vehicle in a public place. You can only drive the type of vehicle for which you hold a license/permit. You must carry your license/permit at all times.

You can take Initial Basic Training courses on vehicles in these licence categories:

Category A
  • IBT Training Course: Motorcycles
  • Motorcycle (All tricycles and light quadricycles)
Category A1
  • Maximum Engine size of 125cc
  • IBT Training Course Small Motorcycles
  • Motorcycle (Tricycles less than 15kW and light quadricycle
Category A2
  • Maximum Power rating of 35Kw
  • IBT Training Course Large Motorcycles
  • 35Kw Restricted (Tricycles less than 15 kW and light quadricycle)
Category AM
  • Maximum Engine size of 50cc
  • IBT Training Course Small Motorcycles
  • Moped (Tricycles with a design speed not exceeding 45km/h Iight quadricycle; whose unladen mass is not more than 400kg and whose maximum net engine power does not exceed 15 kW)
Category B (Code 73)
  • IBT Training Course: Motor Vehicles
  • Quadricycle (For those with certain learner permit restrictions)
